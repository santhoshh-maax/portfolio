Selected work in robotics, automation & software

Automated Plant Monitoring System

Built an Arduino-based system that automatically waters plants based on real-time soil moisture levels. Integrated Twilio SMS notifications to inform users of pump status even without internet.

▶ Video

SIM VITAL - CPR Simulation

Developed an educational medical simulator combining embedded sensors and real-time visualization. Enabled trainees to monitor vital signs while performing CPR on a sensor-enabled mannequin.

Step Assist - Smart IoT Crutch

Designed a smart crutch with sensors for fall detection, step counting, and gait analysis. Built a mobile app with real-time data visualization and voice assistance for elderly users.

Road Bot - Ai Powered Autonomous Rover

An autonomous engineering solution designed to detect and repair road damage in real-time. Utilizing YOLOv8 computer vision and Intel RealSense depth sensing, the rover identifies potholes, calculates exact volumetric repair requirements, and deploys a custom screw-conveyor mixing system for precise concrete application.

Smart Toilet Monitoring System
